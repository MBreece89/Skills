# /grill-me

**Type:** User-invoked  
**Category:** Productivity

## Purpose
Get relentlessly interviewed about a plan or design until every branch of the decision
tree is resolved. Forces you to think through the problem deeply before writing any code.

## When to Use
- Before starting any non-trivial feature or change
- When you have a rough idea but haven't thought it through
- When you want to stress-test your own assumptions

## Steps
Invoke the `grilling` model-invoked skill. See `grilling.md` for the full loop.

## Output
A clear, fully-resolved description of the plan — all ambiguities answered, all branches
considered. This output can be fed directly into `/to-prd` or `/to-issues`.

## Rules
- Do not start building anything during this session.
- Do not accept vague answers — push until the answer is concrete and actionable.
